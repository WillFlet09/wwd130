Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to give ye the best of a Whitewater rafting experience. We want you to come and feel the mighty power of the waves barraging yer vessel and the fight against the river itself. Yer invited to join us on all across Utah to do what the the waters have been calling ye to do: White water raft.

Audience

We're looking for adults of 18 years or older who've got the thrill of adventure but just haven't known where to put it. Intensity is what we've searched all around for and intensity is what we've got. Our locations in Utah are relatively affordable and would make for a great pit stop when going across the country. You can reserve a trip online at this website.
